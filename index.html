ABOUT

Medellin Syndicates started from a group of remote people who have the determination to dominate Las Barancas. This group of people started a small business. Initially the group of businessmen were ex-convicts from Colombia who had the goal of controlling Las Barancas. They do not have many members, only a few people and 5 leaders who are commonly called "The Mad Hatter". Initially one of the leaders named Sergio Silimore wanted to continue his crime, because he himself was unable to continue his crime, so he created an organization called a "syndicate". In continuing his crime Sergio Silimore became a fugitive in Colombia, and he fled to the United States (SA).
